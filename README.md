## 📂 Projects & Methodologies

### 1. Categorical Data Modeling
*Focus: Logistic & Log-Linear GLMs*
* **The Goal:** Analyze drug consumption patterns and medical symptoms using categorical modeling.
* **Key Highlights:** 
    * Implemented Binary and Multinomial Logistic Regression.
    * Diagnosed and corrected **overdispersion** in count data using Negative Binomial models.
    * [Explore Categorical Projects →](Categorical-Data-Modeling/)

### 2. Educational Multilevel Modeling
*Focus: Hierarchical Mixed-Effects Models*
* **The Goal:** Analyze the INVALSI national assessment (Math scores) to understand student performance drivers.
* **Key Highlights:**
    * Accounted for the nested structure of data (Students $\subset$ Classes $\subset$ Schools).
    * Calculated **ICC** (Intraclass Correlation Coefficient) to justify multilevel modeling over OLS.
    * Identified cross-level interaction effects between socio-economic status and regional disparities.
    * [Explore Multilevel Projects →](Educational-Multilevel-Modeling/)

### 3. GAM Literature Review
*Focus: Non-linear Statistical Smoothing*
* **The Goal:** A deep-dive research synthesis on Generalized Additive Models (GAMs).
* **Key Highlights:**
    * Explored the mechanics of **Basis Functions** and B-splines.
    * Compared penalized likelihood approaches vs. black-box machine learning.
    * [Explore Research Reports →](GAM-Literature-Review/)
